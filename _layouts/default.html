Ruijie Fu

I am a graduate student at the CMU Biorobotics Lab. From 2016 to 2020, I studied at Carnegie Mellon University as a math major undergrad. I had a minor in robotics at Robotics Institute, part of the School of Computer Science, where I work on geometric mechanics and SLAM. My research advisor is Howie Choset.

I'm interested in differential geometry, bio-inspired robot locomotion, robot localization and mapping, and computer graphics. My recent work has been on geometric mechanics and sequence matching.
